Tohira Hokkaido Cut Konbu

Scientific name: Saccharina japonica (Family: Laminariaceae)

Common name: Kombu (Kelp), A brown seaweed harvested in Hokkaido, Japan

Ingredients: Dried cut kombu seaweed.

Producer: Tohira Konbu Kalso Co.

Benefits:

  • Rich source of umami flavor used in broths (e.g. dashi)
  • High in dietary fiber
  • Contains minerals, especially iodine
  • Contains fucoidan which may support thyroid function, digestion, and metabolism

Fujicco Benri Wakame Dried Salted Seaweed

Scientific name: Undaria pinnatifida

Common name:Wakame, A brown seaweed

Ingredients: Dried salted wakame seaweed

Producer: Fujicco (Japanese-based producer)

Benefits:

  • Used widely in cooking (soups, salads, and miso dishes)
  • Low-calorie and rich in minerals (iodine, calcium, magnesium) and dietary fiber.
  • Supports thyroid health, digestion, and nutrient density in soups/salads.

Kelp (Tohira Hidaka Konbu)

Scientific name: Saccharina japonica

Common name:Kombu

Ingredients: 100% dried kombu (kelp)

Producer: Tohira (Japanese-style kombul kelp)

Benefits:

  • High in iodine, dietary fiber, and minerals such as calcium and magnesium.
  • Provides natural umami flavor due to the glutamic acid and useful in cooking stocks (dash - japanese soup stock).
  • Support thyroid health and digestion if consume or use in moderate amounts.

Fresh Black Fungus Mushroom

Scientific name: Auricularia polytricha

Common name:Black Fungus Mushroom

Ingredients:

    Pure black fungus mushroom 100%

    Producer: Mx Fruit & Vegetable (M) Sdn. Bhd.

    Benefits:

    • Packed with powerful antioxidants like polyphenols and flavonoids, which help combat oxidative stress in the body and may lower the risk of chronic diseases.
    • Improve blood circulation and help prevent blood platelet aggregation, leading to its traditional use as a "blood purifier" or tonic.
    • Offer protection for the liver from damage caused by certain substances and toxins, a benefit linked to its strong antioxidant properties.
